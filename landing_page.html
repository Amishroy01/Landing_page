Web Development

Web development involves designing and building websites and web applications. It encompasses a wide range of skills, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and backend technologies like Node.js and databases such as MongoDB.

the Future

The future of web development looks promising with the advancement of technologies such as Progressive Web Apps (PWAs), Single Page Applications (SPAs), and serverless architecture. Web developers will continue to play a crucial role in creating responsive, interactive, and user-friendly web experiences across various devices.
